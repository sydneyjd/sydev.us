So,
it has been a rather hectic past couple weeks, due to multiple factors.
First of all, two weeks ago my Grandpa, who i was rather close to, passed away, and it has been dificult for the whole family to re-adjust to not having him.
It has also been rather busy on the farm. We chopped roughly 700 tons of corn and now are starting to combine the drier corn. I love the smell of the corn as we dry it to store in the storage bins.
Among other things, im rather excited about the progress Firefox is making in Electrolysis, the attempt to bring multi-processes to Firefox. In Version 49 which was released 3 weeks ago, they have it enabled for 100% of users who don't have addons or have known electrolysis compatable addons. For the testing i've done, it makes Firefox a much perkier browser that can now handle these extremely overloaded JavaScript websites with ease. YAY!!
Other than that, i think that wraps up this blog post, im going to have to improve on how i do this, but over time i will learn. Thats how we all learn, or so i think.
